Integration Points (so the 3 modules work as one system)


Data flowing out of Competency/LMS → into Talent Management
	Data flowing out of Talent Management → into Competency/LMS
	Competency gap scores → feed Targeted Training Plan assignment (LMS) and inform promotion/succession readiness (TM)
	New hire's role/job-profile → triggers Competency framework mapping & onboarding learning path
	Job-role-to-competency matrix → used by AI resume screening to weight candidate fit
	Performance/goal outcomes → feed 9-box/talent-pool placement (owned by Competency)
	Skills-to-course mapping (LMS) → powers auto-recommended learning after a TM-identified gap or new role
	Promotion/transfer event → triggers re-mapping of employee's required competency set
	This keeps each module owning one job: Competency = define & measure skill, LMS = build skill, Talent Management = acquire, place, evaluate, reward, and move talent based on that data—with no duplicated build of framework, ratings, or course delivery.
